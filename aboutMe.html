About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineering student at the Colorado School of Mines with a minor in Robotics & Intelligent Systems, pursuing a Masters in Robotics. My current work focuses on distributed robotics and resilient multi-agent systems. At the MPALA Lab, I focus on developing aerial swarm platforms with Crazyflie drones, OptiTrack motion capture, and ROS 2 to test consensus and resilient control algorithms under real-world conditions.
