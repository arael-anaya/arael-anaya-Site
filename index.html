Robotics Research in Control and Multi-Agent Systems

I design and experimentally validate resilient multi-agent robotic systems, with a focus on aerial swarms, distributed control, and real-world autonomy. I am a robotics graduate student at Colorado School of Mines, and my work bridges theoretical guarantees with hands-on implementation using Crazyflie drones, motion capture, and ROS 2.

  • Real-World Autonomy
    Bridging theoretical guarantees with hardware-in-the-loop robotic testing.
