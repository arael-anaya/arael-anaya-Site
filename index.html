Robotics, Control, and Multi-Agent Systems

I design and experimentally validate resilient multi-agent robotic systems, with a focus on aerial swarms, distributed control, and real-world autonomy. My work bridges theoretical guarantees with hands-on implementation using Crazyflie drones, motion capture, and ROS 2.
