Portable Pedal-Powered Generator

MEGN 301 Final Design Project · Fall 2025

Designed and manufactured a portable mechanical generator capable of producing usable DC power for phone charging and lighting. The system integrates a custom transmission, back-driven stepper motor, power electronics, and user interface. Emphasis was placed on affordability, manufacturability, ergonomics, and real-world performance validation.

Accessible Cup Holder Bracket

MEGN 381 · Manufacturing Processes · Fall 2025

Designed, analyzed, and manufactured a sheet-metal steel cup holder bracket for wheelchair and crutch users. The project emphasized design for manufacturability, structural validation, and scalable production.

  • Sheet-metal design with plasma cutting, bending, and welding
  • FEA-based structural validation and factor-of-safety analysis
  • Cost modeling and time studies for production scaling
  • Integration of resin-printed polymer components
